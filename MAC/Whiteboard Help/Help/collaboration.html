How to Connect and Collaborate

Whiteboard Pro: Collaborative Drawing for Mac supports collaboration over local Wi-Fi

Whiteboard uses Bonjour to discover devices running Whiteboard for iOS on the local network (usually Wi-Fi).

  1. Download and install Whiteboard on both your Mac and iOS device. Whiteboard supports every iOS device, including the iPhone, iPod touch, and iPad.
  2. Connect your Mac and your iOS device to the same Wi-Fi network.
  3. Launch Whiteboard on your Mac and on your iOS device.
  4. On your Mac, ensure the Networking button is active.
  5. On your iOS device, click the Connect button, wait for the name of your Mac to appear, and then tap it to connect.

Enjoy the wireless collaborative drawing experience!
